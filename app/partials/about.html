Basic Scenario

We all do money transaction in various form or activity and we want to track those transaction. We might want some transaction to be tracked as personal and some transaction as group or same transaction as personal and group depending on scenario.

First Scenario: Personal tracking
You want to track your income, expense or transfer. On what item you expense more, to whom and how much you have lend....etc
Final Objective: How much, when, on what, where; expense, income or transfer.

Second Scenario: Group tracking
Suppose you live in PG and share the expense with your friends where each member buy some items at different time and expense is shared by each member of the group.
Final Objective: How much each member will give to or get from other member.
